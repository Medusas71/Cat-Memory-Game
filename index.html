Cat Memory Game
Instructions

How good is your memory? Can you match the cards correctly?

Start the game by clicking a card. The card flips and an image of a cat displays. Click a different card and see if the images match. There are always two identical images somewhere in the game.

If the images match, the cards stay faceup.

If the images donâ€™t match, both cards stay faceup until you click another card. You can exit the game at any time by clicking the Exit button on the screen or you can restart the game.

Once you have found all the matching cards you can either continue on to the next level or you can exit the game. Currently there are 2 levels with more levels coming soon!
